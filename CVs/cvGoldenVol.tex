There is a fundamental difference between software that simply functions and software that respects its users' time. When it comes to non-profit organizations and volunteer networks, having access to reliable, intuitive tools directly impacts their quality of life. Volunteers give the most valuable resource they have — their time — and they deserve tools built with excellence. This mission is exactly why I am tracking Golden’s growth, and why I want to offer my strengths to your support operations.

My professional DNA is rooted in community management and user advocacy. Over a 14 year career spanning major digital platforms, high sensitivity executive forums, and years of early independent forum administration, I have learned how to manage high volume user spaces with empathy and consistency. I know what it means to look at a chaotic system, identify where users are hitting a wall, and build the clear documentation or response workflows needed to fix it.

Currently, I serve as a Customer Experience and Patient Registration Technician at a high volume health center, while also working closely with the community and volunteer docents at the Air Zoo Aviation Museum. I have long recognized that these docents contribute an immense amount of time and expertise, and they deserve reliable, modern tools to support the vital work they do. I have always been committed to building the kind of tools and support systems that respect and empower that level of dedication.

My experience means I am sharp, quick on my feet, and highly capable of managing difficult human-centric situations under pressure. I know how to turn a frustrated interaction into a positive one, ensuring that every person who reaches out feels recognized and valued. This is a standard I hold for every project I take on.

What sets me apart as a support professional is my technical baseline. Because I completed an intensive full-stack web development program, I don't just log tickets — I understand how software is built. I can speak the language of your engineering team, read database logic, and help isolate whether a user’s friction is a frontend layout issue or a backend schema discrepancy. I am eager to bring this combined toolkit to a dedicated, asynchronous remote environment where clear technical communication and independent problem solving are the standard.

I am an enthusiastic, self-starting individual who wants to fully engage with a product I believe in. I would love the opportunity to bring my experience in community operations and technical triage to the Golden team, protecting your developers' bandwidth while genuinely brightening your users' day.
